Step 1

Screening

We ensure all our Tutors have at least 2 years of teaching experience in the English subject, hold (at least) a Degree in English or Education and attain a minimum English proficiency of CEFR C2/ IELTS Band 8.5.

Step 2

Interview with Tutor Management Team

We identify the Tutor’s core motives to ensure we have the most dedicated Tutors.

Step 3

Mock Online Lesson

GuruLab ensures each Tutor delivers effective and engaging lessons according to the GuruLab Methodology.

Step 4

Interview with Senior Leadership Team

We evaluate their critical thinking and problem-solving skills to deal with the unique needs of each learner.

Step 5

Background Screening

We want to ensure a safe learning environments for learners, so all Tutors are subject to a background screening before we appoint them.
