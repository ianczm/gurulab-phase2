Step 1

Complete Sign Up Form

Fill up the short sign up form so that our Education Consultant can reach out to you to understand your child’s needs better!

Step 2

Personal Consultation Call

After you submit the form, our Education Consultant will contact you to provide recommendations based on the information you provided.

Step 3

Class-Level Allocation

Our Education Consultant will recommend the right class for your child and check with you on a class timing that works.

Step 4

Class Registration and Enrolment

Once you confirm the time of the class, fill up the Class Enrolment Form and complete the payment of your child’s fee to confirm your slot for the class depending on the package you select. Your child is now an official GuruLab Learner!

Step 5

Pre-class Placement Test

Before your child attends their first lesson, they will need to take a free Placement Test to identify which areas in English they need the most support in.

Step 6

Attend GuruLab Classes

Learners will learn all content specially curated using GuruLab’s unique methodology and will be able to access all features of the GuruLab app such as class links, class recordings, assignment feedback, attendance tracker, assignment tracker and more. All learners’ assignments will be marked and provided with comprehensive feedback by their tutors to improve further.

Step 7

Class Assessment

Learners will take a Progress Test every 14 weeks to evaluate their progress and to identify areas for improvement and ways to tackle them.

Step 8

Progress Test Report

Once the Progress Test results are out, you will receive a detailed analysis of your child’s progress and detailed feedback from their tutor.
