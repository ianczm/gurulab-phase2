CEFR-aligned

We align everything that we teach to international standards (CEFR A2 to C1) with the help of curriculum specialists so that our graduates are ready to be global citizens.

Exam-ready

With core skills tested in school exams covered in our English Essentials programme and free seminars on exam formats in UPSR English, PT3 English, SPM English, UEC English, IGCSE English, our curriculum truly goes above and beyond school syllabi.

Suitable for all learning needs

Whether your child is a Form 5 student struggling to write SPM English essays without glaring grammar mistakes or a Form 3 student wanting to learn more advanced writing skills beyond the PT3 English syllabus, there is something for everyone!
